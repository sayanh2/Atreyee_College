About Us

This project will help us to track any student of Engineering College. Here three people can operate the site 1. Mentor 2. Student 3. Admin . Mentor can track students, he / she can give them marks , rating ,..etc. He can also upload student's attendance. Student can view their profile, semester wise performance, they can edit their details etc. Admin can change mentor name semester wise .
